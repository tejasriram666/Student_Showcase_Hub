Virtual Reality & Augmented Reality

Coming to the amazing new technology 2022, i.e., VR & AR. These two technologies are also dominating the current market. Virtual Reality immerses the user in an environment while Augmented Reality improves their environment. Although these technologies are majorly used in the gaming industry, they have also been utilized for training software developed for US Navy and more. Professionals state that AR and VR have tremendous potential in education, marketing, training, and entertainment. Around 14 million AR and VR devices were sold in 2019, which is expected to rise to $209.2 billion by 2022. These trending solutions create more opportunities and welcome all-scale businesses to prepare for this game-changing field.
